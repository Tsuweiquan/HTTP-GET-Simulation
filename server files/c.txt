	<p style="text-align: justify;">The <span class="style2 style10">Computer Engineering</span> programme(CEG) at NUS prepares its graduates to embark on a lifelong journey in designing computing systems for a smarter world â€“ hence our theme <span class="style2 style10">"Designing Intelligence"</span>.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">A sophisticated piece of hardware is useless without the software that <span class="style2 style10">brings it to life</span>. Computer engineers introduce intelligence into every conceivable device --- from the smart phone that you cannot live without, to massive industrial control systems that power economies. They create the electronic systems in a modern car containing dozens of computing systems communicating through a network. They connect the physical world with cyberspace to enhance everything from entertainment to healthcare and the environment. CEG gives you the skills and knowledge to <span class="style2 style10">engineer exciting solutions</span> that move as well as change the world.</p>
<p style="text-align: justify;">A uniquely <span class="style2 style10">multi-disciplinary</span> programme, CEG transcends the traditional boundary of computer science and electrical engineering. The curriculum aims to bring real-world problems, solutions, and experiences into the university environment. You will have the opportunity to consolidate your knowledge through a unique <span class="style2 style10">Full-Year industrial attachment</span>, and through overseas work or learning experience. With the solid fundamentals that the NUS Computer Engineering degree will give you, only imagination and ambition will be your limits.</p>
